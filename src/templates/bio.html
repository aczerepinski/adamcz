Adam Czerepinski - Bio

Adam Czerepinski was born and raised on the east side of Madison, WI, and after twenty years living on the east coast, once again calls Madison home.

Proficient as a multi-instrumentalist, Adam is a versatile keyboard and bass player, and one of the ok-est trumpet players in town.

As a teenager, Adam attended the prestigious Berklee College of Music on a full scholarship. After receiving his bachelor's degree in Jazz Composition he continued his studies at the Manhattan School of Music where he earned a master's degree in Jazz Trumpet and a Doctor of Musical Arts in Jazz Arts Advancement.
